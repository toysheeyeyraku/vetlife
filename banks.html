OUR Clinic

Vetlife Banks Peninsula is a genuine mixed animal practice, offering services from dairying, beef, sheep, and working dogs, right through to all types of companion animals and pets including birds.

Services available from our Little River clinic include Pregnancy diagnosis, nutrition and drenching advice for large animals, while companion animals enjoy in-house surgery (including orthopaedic), an x-ray facility, dental services and surgery, a range of special diets, and a clipping/grooming service. On Monday afternoons mobile clinics are held from 2pm in Akaroa and on Wednesday afternoons clinics are held from 2pm in Diamond Harbour.

With 3 veterinarians, Vetlife Banks Peninsula offers 24/7 veterinary services to all companion and production animals. Please feel free to give us a call about all your animal needs and questions.
